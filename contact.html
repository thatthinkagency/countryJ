Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about Country J and his music

Tickets for upcoming dates are available through our official website, Ticketmaster, and authorized resellers. Check our Tour page for the latest availability and direct ticket links. We recommend purchasing from official sources to avoid scams.

Yes! We love performing at weddings, corporate events, festivals, and private parties. Visit our Bookings page to submit a request. Our team will review your inquiry and get back to you with availability and pricing within 24 hours.

Country J's music is available on all major streaming platforms including Spotify, Apple Music, YouTube Music, Amazon Music, and Deezer. You can also purchase physical copies and digital downloads through our official store and major retailers.

While we can't guarantee specific song requests during live performances (as setlists are carefully planned), we love hearing from fans! Send us your song suggestions and stories through our contact form, and we'll consider them for future shows and recordings.

Follow Country J on social media (@countryj on Instagram, TikTok, and YouTube) and sign up for our newsletter using the form at the bottom of any page. We'll also send updates about new releases, tour announcements, and exclusive content.

Meet & greets are available for VIP ticket holders and can sometimes be arranged for special events. Check our Tour page for VIP packages, or inquire about meet & greet availability when purchasing tickets. We always try to connect with fans when possible!

While Country J doesn't currently offer formal music lessons, we occasionally host songwriting workshops and masterclasses. Follow us on social media for announcements about upcoming events, and check our Bookings page for workshop availability.

Use the contact form above to send us your question! We read every message and do our best to respond promptly. For booking inquiries, visit our Bookings page. For press inquiries, check our Press page.
