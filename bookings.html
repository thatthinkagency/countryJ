Technical Rider - Country J
Stage & Sound Requirements
  • Stage: Minimum 20'x15' performance area, 8' clearance above stage
  • Sound System: House system with 4-6 microphones, monitor system
  • Lighting: Basic stage lighting, follow spots for lead vocals
  • Backline: Full drum kit, bass amp, guitar amps provided by artist
Accommodations
  • Dressing Room: Private room with bathroom, adequate lighting
  • Catering: 6 assorted sandwiches, fresh fruit, bottled water, soft drinks
  • Transportation: Round-trip airport transfer for artist and band
  • Security: One security personnel for load-in/load-out
Load-in/Load-out
  • Load-in: 4 hours before show time
  • Sound Check: 1 hour before show time
  • Load-out: Within 2 hours after show
  • Stage Access: Clear path for equipment movement
Note: This is a summary of key requirements. Full technical rider available upon booking confirmation.
