From Small Town Roots to Country Music Royalty

Born and raised in Buffalo, New York, Country J grew up with the sounds of honky-tonk legends echoing through local venues and family gatherings. What started as weekend gigs at local bars has evolved into a career defined by authenticity, heart, and an unwavering commitment to the traditional country values that shaped him.

After years of grinding through the New York music scene, Country J began his journey as a country artist in 2018. Armed with nothing but his guitar, a notebook full of songs, and an unshakeable belief in the power of real country music, he began writing what would become his breakout material.

His debut single "Dirt Road Dreams" became an overnight sensation, topping the Texas charts and earning critical acclaim for its raw storytelling and genuine emotion. Since then, Country J has released two studio albums, toured extensively across North America, and collaborated with some of country's most respected names.
