Born in Buffalo, New York - A Real Story to Tell

Born in Buffalo, New York, Country J carries a real story that one day you'll hear all over - incredible and a blessing. It's a story of hardships and overcoming dangers in life that many people can relate with. This authentic journey shaped the heart behind his real, authentic, heartfelt, and fun music.

From grinding through life's challenges to finding his voice in country music, Country J began his journey as a country artist in 2018. Armed with nothing but his guitar, a notebook full of songs born from real experiences, and an unshakeable belief in the power of real country music, he began writing what would become his breakout material.

His debut single "Dirt Road Dreams" became an overnight sensation, topping the Texas charts and earning critical acclaim for its raw storytelling and genuine emotion. Since then, Country J has released two studio albums, toured extensively across North America, and collaborated with some of country's most respected names.
